MESD Documentation LMS-30-View-all-enrolled-subjects
Moodle provides students with a user-friendly interface to access and manage their enrolled courses.
Usage
1.	Login to Moodle:
•	Open your web browser and go to your institution's Moodle site.
•	Enter your username and password to log in.
2.	Navigate to Course or My Courses:
•	Check the main site navigation menu for a link named "Courses" or "My Courses." This may lead you to a page displaying all the courses you are enrolled in.
